How the economy is adapting to technology

The economy is always changing and growing, however the recent world events of the pandemic combined with the technology that is available to everyone has changed how the business function successfully. Uber and Lyft revolutionized the taxi service, when is the last time that you heard someone say they were getting a cab not an uber? This technological push of business has been accelerated by the pandemic and social distancing. While many stores have more restrictions and guidelines, online shopping has increased in popularity. For many people that wanted to stay home, or were quarantined and still wanted to purchase things, amazon became the go to source for all their shopping needs. Amazon who has been successful in dominating the online shopping area for some time now, has gotten even bigger during the pandemic, as for many people their only option was to order things online. When it comes to ordering things, online Amazon is the clear choice, with amazon prime, people get free two-day shipping, if not shorter. If businesses want to be able to compete with Amazon, they need to have an efficient place to order their products and efficient shipping for their products.
