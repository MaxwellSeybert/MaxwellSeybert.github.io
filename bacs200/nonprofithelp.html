Want to Help the Food Bank?

It could be easier than you think!

>

While many may thing that the biggest cost for a food bank is the food, that is not the case. While the food bank gets many donations of food and cheap access to it by buying in volume, money is needed to transport and distrubute food. Giving money is an easy way to help people get access to the food that they need. Interested in donating?

Donate Here!

While a food bank can get food for cheap by purchasing in volume, more food means more people that can be fed. It is easy to help by donating food! Anybody can drop off food at the food bank and it will be distributed to the people who need it.
