How the Weld Food Bank is helping.

The Weld Food Bank has a vision to help the hungry in Weld County, and is doing so with a number of programs.

Mobile Food Pantry

The mobile food pantry allows for more people to be helped. By taking food straight to the people that need it, these people gain easy access to the foods that they need.

Backpack Program

Many students in Weld County are qualified for free or reduced lunch at school. Since schools are not in session on the weekends, these students' access this benefit is gone. The backpack program gives these students a bag of food for the weekend. This gives these students access to food and confidence that they will eat over the weekend.

School Pantry

The Weld Food Bank provides food for students and their families. The food bank provides breakfast to a student who is hungry and also gives the family of the student food after school.

Emergency Food Boxes

The Weld Food Bank gives people emeregency food boxes when they are in need of food. These food boxes contain enough food for a family of 4 for 3 days and are designed by a nutritionist.
