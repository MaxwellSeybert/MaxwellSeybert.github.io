Social Media and Extremeism

Pros

  • Social media allows people to communicate faster
  • Allows people to meet more people and make more connections
  • Creates an easy way to keep existing connections

Cons

  • Creates superficial connections
  • Increase of offensive and hurtful material
  • Can create Laziness in keeping up with connections

While social media can be great in allowing people to stay in touch with others or even create new connections, it does have drawbacks. For example, while not everyone on the internet is hateful, there exist many people that enjoy hurting others and bringing them dowm. These people can be very active on the internet and cause many people to be hurt. Social meadia can also make people feel ostracized, since there are people with wildly huge follower counts in the millions, a person who does not have a large online presence may feel like they are a less important as a person.

Pros

  • Social media allows for companies to better connect with their customers
  • Brands have an easier and streamlined way to announce and inform the public about their products
  • Social media platforms like Youtube allow for people to create careers out of things that would typically be much more difficult to get a career in

Cons

  • Social Media has increased the amount of fake/scam businesses since anybody can make an account and start posting
  • The presence of advertising has increased exponentially
  • Peoples private data is being used for targeted advertising

Social media is great for businesses and consumers alike as both now have a better means of communication with eachother rather than either calling a call center to get help or going to a retail store in hopes for assistance. Now people can just tweet at a brands' support team and get help relativley quickly. Things like YouTube have changed the economy as many people want to and viable could be a "Youtuber" in which they make videos about something they are passionate about. However, social media has also increased the chances that someone falls victim to a scam. Since anyone can make a social media account and pretend to be a business, scams have increased.
